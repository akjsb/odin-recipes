Jackstraw Tuna Casserole

Description

A simple but flavorful casserole, this is sure to be a hit when you don't have a lot of time to spend in the kitchen. Shoestring potatoes provide a nice crunch when it is fresh from the oven. It's like a snack casserole.

    Ingredients
  • 4 oz can shoestring potatoes
  • 1 11oz can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 6oz can tuna, drained
  • 6oz evaporated milk

    Directions
  1. Reserve about 1 cup of shoestring potatoes and set aside.
  2. Combine the rest of the ingredients in casserole dish and mix well
  3. Sprinkle remaining shoestring potatoes on the top
  4. Bake, uncovered at 375 degrees Fahrenheit for about 25-30 mins
